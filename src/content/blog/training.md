## üèÉ Day 1: Speed & Rotational Power

*Focus: Explosive movements and core rotation.*

* **Dynamic Warm-up (10 mins):** High knees, butt kicks, leg swings, arm circles.
* **60-Foot Sprints:** 6 reps. (Full speed from home to first). Walk back for recovery.
* **5-10-5 Pro-Agility Shuttle:** 4 reps. Sprint 5 yards right, 10 yards left, 5 yards back to center.
* **Med Ball Rotational Side Toss:** 3 sets of 8 reps per side. (4-6lb ball). Focus on "loading" the back hip and firing through the core.
* **Med Ball Overhead Slams:** 3 sets of 10 reps. Reach high on toes and slam the ball down hard.

---

## üí™ Day 2: Strength & Arm Care

*Focus: Building the "Engine" (legs) and protecting the shoulder.*

* **Goblet Squats:** 3 sets of 12 reps. Hold weight at chest level; keep heels flat.
* **Forward & Lateral Lunges:** 3 sets of 10 reps per leg. (Step forward, then step out to the side).
* **J-Bands / Resistance Band Rotations:** 2 sets of 15 reps (Internal and External rotation).
* **Y-W-T Holds:** 3 sets of 10 reps. Lie on stomach, lift arms to form letters Y, W, and T. Hold each for 2 seconds.
* **Plank with Shoulder Taps:** 3 sets of 45 seconds. Keep hips level.

## ‚ö° Day 4: Agility & Lateral Power

*Focus: The side-to-side power needed for the pitching mound.*

* **Lateral Bounds:** 3 sets of 10 reps. Jump side-to-side, landing softly and holding balance.
* **Skaters:** 3 sets of 12 reps. Fast, explosive side-to-side jumps.
* **Med Ball Step-Into Lateral Toss:** 3 sets of 8 reps per side. Mimic your pitching stride while throwing the ball sideways.
* **Dead Bugs:** 3 sets of 15 reps. Keep your lower back pressed firmly into the floor.

## ‚öæ Day 5: Pitching Strength & Core

*Focus: Single-leg stability and mechanical consistency.*

* **Bulgarian Split Squats:** 3 sets of 10 reps per leg. (Back foot elevated on a bench or chair).
* **Single-Leg Romanian Deadlift:** 3 sets of 8 reps per leg. Balance on one leg, hinge at hips, touch the floor.
* **Towel Drill:** 25 reps. Use a hand towel to go through your full pitching motion; focus on a strong finish.
* **Russian Twists:** 3 sets of 20 reps.
* **Glute Bridges:** 3 sets of 15 reps. Squeeze glutes at the top.

## üí° Expert Notes

* **Hydration:** Drink half your body weight in ounces of water daily.
* **Pain vs. Soreness:** Muscle soreness is okay. Sharp pain in the elbow or front of the shoulder means **STOP** and rest.
